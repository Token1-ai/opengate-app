Never made a token before? Here's exactly what happens:

1. Logo (optional) — upload any picture. It automatically gets cropped to a square and resized, so you don't need to prepare it yourself.

2. Name — the full name of your token, e.g. "My Awesome Token".

3. Symbol — a short ticker, usually 3–5 letters, e.g. "MAT". This is what shows up next to the amount in wallets.

4. Decimals — how finely your token can be divided. 18 is the standard almost everyone uses (same as BNB itself) — leave it unless you have a specific reason to change it.

5. Total Supply — how many tokens exist in total, right from the start. E.g. 1,000,000. All of them go straight to your wallet when the token is created.

6. Optional features — toggle any of these on:
• Mintable — you can create more tokens later, whenever you want.
• Burnable — anyone holding the token can permanently destroy their own tokens (often used to reduce supply over time).
• Pausable — you can freeze all transfers instantly in an emergency (e.g. if something goes wrong), and unfreeze later.
• Tax — a small % of every transfer is automatically redirected to your wallet (common for project funding). You choose the %.
• Anti-Whale — blocks any single transfer larger than a % you set of the total supply, to slow down bots dumping right after launch.

7. Currency — pick BNB, USDT or USDC to pay the creation fee.

8. Hit "Create Token" and confirm in your wallet. Your token is a real, independent contract — fully yours, works in any wallet, and you're free to list it on any exchange or DEX (like PancakeSwap) yourself, add liquidity, and share it with the world.

✅ Verified on BscScan automatically — every token created here shows up on BscScan with its source code already verified (the green checkmark), so anyone can see exactly how it works. You don't need to do anything for this — it happens on its own.
